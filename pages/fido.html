Fido

Fido is a mobile network operator, is used by lot of international students including me and many other people. I only redesign the UI part of this application to give it unique and attractive look.

What changes I made in UI

In Fido mobile application, in 5 feature screens, I make the changes in color collection, typography, iconography, and buttons.

I make the header and footer bar of dark purple color and change the colors of buttons to dark purple and peach, I make the buttons little small.

I give the text color according to background of the screen, where back color is dark then text color is white and on the light back screen text color is dark purple on all the screens. I use the montserrat font type with bold, semibold, medium and regular styles.

I give the cards to differentiate between each section, some cards have only stroke of dark purple color and some cards have white area and some cards have peach background color.

I change all the icons style, I choose dark purple and white color icons for all the screens. I give some unique icons to the application.
