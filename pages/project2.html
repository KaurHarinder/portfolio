Project Beerfond

Find your perfect beer

Beerfond website helps the user to find a best pair of food and beer by nearby locations of restaurants. This is a best way to finding the best type of beer, the ideal meal pairing and a nice place to enjoy beer.

Target Audience

People from age 25 years to 55 years.



Main features

Customization Suggestions, After setting some preferences of yours a list of beer type will be displayed for you to pick from.

Explore, A map of Vancouver will be displayed with all the major local breweries with a picture and address of each location.

Reservation, Book a table in advance on your favorite breweries.

My role

As a designer, I did all the designing work in this project with other designers as a group member. I designed the Userflow, Wireframes, UI Kit, Mockups, Prototype, Proposal, Presentaion to complete this project.
